The Blues Man

Lee Chambers 

 

She left him with a broken heart

He walks alone, confused.

Lost without her by his side

He softly sings the blues. 


 

In the darkened stormy night

He sings his solemn tune.

Tears roll from his reddened eyes

As he strums, and drums, and croons. 


  

The joyful music he once had sang

Only causes him to weep.

He thinks of the love he once had,

And how he set her free.  





  

His fear of loss caused him to lose

The only love he's ever known,

And now he has nowhere to go,

Her arms were once his home.  





  

So he strolls down an empty street

In search of a reason to hold on.

She is now his only muse,

And her memory is his song.
